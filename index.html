WHAT WE DO ?

Speech Therapy

We provide speech therapies for fluency disorders and resonance disorders along with articulation and expressive disorders. These therapies are conducted one-to-one by friendly speech-language pathologists.

PTA

We perform the Pure Tone Audiometry(PTA) procedure to determine auditory sensitivity and to find out the type and amount of hearing loss by producing audiograms.

OAE

Oto Acoustic Emission (OAE) test is conducted to measure the wellness of the inner ear or cochlea and its response to sound. This session includes a screening using audiometer.

Tympanometry

Tympanometry is a simple test that can help diagnose problems related to hearing loss. The test shows how well the middle ear works by measuring the eardrum movements.
